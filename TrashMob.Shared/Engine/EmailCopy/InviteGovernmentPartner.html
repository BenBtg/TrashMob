TrashMob.eco is a non-profit organization that helps communities organize and schedule litter cleanups. The service is completely free for both communities and citizens. That you are getting this email means that someone in your community has joined TrashMob.eco, and wants your community to be a TrashMob.eco partner community!

What does being a TrashMob.eco Partner Community Mean?

When it comes to volunteers cleaning up litter, there are a number of logistical challenges that TrashMob.eco helps the volunteers to overcome:

  • Each volunteer with TrashMob.eco signs an online waiver to cover liability.
  • As a 501(c)(3) volunteer organization, we can sign Organizational Volunteer Agreements with communities so that the volunteers don't have to submit volunteer waivers with the city meaning less paperwork for your community to manage.
  • TrashMob.eco provides a way for partners to declare which services you want to provide the volunteers, and automates the process by which users can request these services. Services could include:
    • Safety Equipment and Supplies
    • Starter Kits
    • Litter Hauling
    • Litter Disposal

So how do I get started as a TrashMob.eco Partner?

It takes just a few minutes of your time to get started:

  1. Create an Account on TrashMob.eco
  2. Fill out and submit the Become a Partner form
  3. A TrashMob.eco will process your application and send you a response with instructions on how to move forward!
